AMRITSAR

Introduction


Amritsar, the spiritual heart of Punjab, is a city steeped in history and devotion. Founded by Guru Ram Das in the 16th century, it is home to the world-famous Golden Temple, a symbol of peace and equality. The city reflects a vibrant blend of Sikh culture, warm hospitality, and rich traditions. From its sacred sites to its bustling streets, Amritsar offers a soulful experience of faith, food, and heritage.

Glimpses of Amritsar's History


  • Founded in 1577 by Guru Ram Das, the fourth Sikh Guru.
  • Home to the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), the holiest shrine of Sikhism.
  • Known as the spiritual and cultural center of the Sikh religion.
  • Famous for the Jallianwala Bagh massacre (1919) — a key event in India's freedom struggle.

Amritsar's Culture


  • Deeply rooted in Sikh traditions and values, centered around the Golden Temple.
  • People are known for their warmth, hospitality, and strong community spirit.
  • Bhangra and Giddha are popular traditional dances performed during festivals.
  • Traditional attire includes vibrant phulkari dupattas, turbans, and salwar kameez.
  • Festivals like Baisakhi, Gurpurab, Lohri, and Diwali are celebrated with great enthusiasm.
  • Cuisine is rich and flavorful — famous dishes include Amritsari kulcha, sarson da saag, and lassi.

Best Time To Visit

Peak Season (October to March)

  • 1. Pleasant and cool weather, perfect for sightseeing.
  • 2. Ideal time to visit the Golden Temple, Wagah Border, and other attractions comfortably.
  • 3. Major festivals like Diwali, Guru Nanak Jayanti, and Lohri are celebrated with great enthusiasm.
  • 4. Hotel rates are higher and places are crowded due to heavy tourist inflow.

Moderate Season (April - June)

  • 1. Warm weather but still manageable for sightseeing, especially in mornings and evenings.
  • 2. Lesser crowd compared to the winter months, making temple visits peaceful.
  • 3. Good time to explore indoor attractions like the Partition Museum.
  • 4. Better hotel deals and availability than peak season.

Off-Season (July - September)

  • 1. Monsoon months bring humidity and occasional rain, reducing outdoor comfort.
  • 2. Fewer tourists, offering a calm and quiet experience around major sites.
  • 3. Hotel and travel prices drop significantly, making
  • 4. Best for budget travelers and indoor sightseeing.
