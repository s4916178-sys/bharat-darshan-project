Kolkata's Culture


  • Kolkata is known as the Cultural Capital of India.
  • The city is famous for its art, literature, music, and theatre.
  • It is home to great personalities like Rabindranath Tagore and Satyajit Ray.
  • The most famous festival celebrated here is Durga Puja.
  • People also celebrate Kali Puja, Saraswati Puja, and Pohela Boishakh (Bengali New Year) with joy.
  • Kolkata is known for its traditional Bengali food and sweets like Rasgulla and Sandesh.
  • The city beautifully blends tradition with modern lifestyle and creativity.

Attractions

Howrah Bridge

An iconic bridge and one of the busiest cantilever bridges in the world.

Victoria Memorial

A grand marble monument built in memory of Queen Victoria.

Dakshineswar Kali Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kali on the banks of the Hooghly River.

Belur Math

The headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission, known for its peaceful atmosphere.

A One of the largest planetariums in Asia, offering fascinating space shows.

Cuisines Of Kolkata
